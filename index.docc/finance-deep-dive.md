The Robinhood financial deep dive should emphasize market data streams, order matching, and trading
constraints. These are the core LeetCode problems to anchor the practice set:

- <doc:apple-gaming-leetcode-1801-number-of-orders-in-the-backlog> (order book matching)
- <doc:apple-gaming-leetcode-901-online-stock-span> (price streaks + monotonic stack)
- <doc:apple-gaming-leetcode-502-ipo> (capital allocation under constraints)
- <doc:apple-gaming-leetcode-121-best-time-stock> (single transaction baseline)
