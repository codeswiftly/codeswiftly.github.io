## 24-Hour iOS App Runbook

Goal: complete every prompt at least once, end-to-end, on a timer, while narrating tradeoffs.

### Timebox (Per Prompt)

1) 3 minutes: restate the problem, clarify constraints, state the invariant you will maintain.
2) 7 minutes: write 3–6 tests (happy path + edge + one “trap” case).
3) 25 minutes: implement.
4) 5 minutes: complexity, 2 alternatives, 3 edge cases you tested.
