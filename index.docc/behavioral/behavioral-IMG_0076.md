Questions about weaknesses or mistakes are tests of self-awareness and growth. Answer them with a
real example, not a disguised strength.

## What Interviewers Want

- Honest reflection on a real mistake.
- Evidence of learning and behavioral change.
- Emotional maturity and accountability.

## How to Respond

- Admit a real weakness or mistake.
- Explain the impact.
- Describe what you changed and how it improved your work.
