# Tailoring Questions to the Interviewer


Different interviewers can answer different types of questions. Target your questions so you get
the highest-signal information.

## Hiring Manager

- Team priorities and success metrics.
- Management philosophy and growth support.

## Team Members

- Day-to-day reality and technical challenges.
- Collaboration patterns and culture signals.

## Cross-functional Partners

- Decision-making process and communication patterns.
- Vision and priorities across the org.

## Recruiters

- Process, timeline, and logistics.

## Senior Leadership

- Company strategy, culture, and long-term direction.
