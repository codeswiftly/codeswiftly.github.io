# Organizational and Cultural Challenge Prompts


Behavioral questions often probe your ability to navigate team dynamics and organizational
complexity. Prepare stories that address these common areas.

## Team Operations Challenges

- Onboarding or ramping new teammates.
- Advocating for headcount, resources, or recognition.
- Resolving interpersonal conflict within the team.
- Distributing high-visibility work fairly.

## Org-level Challenges

- Knowledge transfer gaps after departures.
- Communication breakdowns across teams.
- Misalignment between team and company priorities.
- Process drag with resistance to change.
- Unclear decision authority.
- Reorgs or leadership changes creating uncertainty.
