# Conflict Story Example: Quantify Risk and Negotiate Compromise


Strong conflict resolution uses evidence to reframe stakes and to reach a balanced decision.

## What to Emulate

- Quantify technical risk in business terms.
- Offer a concrete compromise instead of just pushing back.
- Identify a staged approach that reduces risk while keeping momentum.

## Signals You Demonstrate

- Judgment under pressure.
- Ability to influence cross-functional decisions.
- Willingness to share the burden of the solution.
