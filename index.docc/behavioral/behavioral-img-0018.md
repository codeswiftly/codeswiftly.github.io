# The Big Three Behavioral Questions (Why They Are Predictable)


Most loops include the same three behavioral prompts. This is your chance to prepare answers that
showcase your strongest signals without being surprised.

## The Three Questions

- Tell me about yourself.
- Tell me about your most impactful project.
- Tell me about a conflict you resolved.

## How to Use Them Strategically

- Pick stories that reinforce your top 2 to 3 strengths.
- Use these answers to steer follow-up questions toward your best material.
- Keep the opening answers concise so the interviewer has room to dig deeper.
