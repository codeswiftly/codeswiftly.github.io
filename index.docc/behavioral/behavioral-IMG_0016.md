Expect probing follow-ups. Prepare your story so you can answer with clarity and confidence under
pressure.

## Contribution Questions

- What was your specific contribution?
- How much was your idea versus assigned work?
- Who else was involved and what did they do?

## Decision Questions

- How did you make the decision?
- What alternatives did you consider?
- Who else influenced the decision?

## Results Questions

- How did you measure success?
- What was the long-term impact?
- What would you do differently?

## Relationship Questions

- How did others respond to your approach?
- What feedback did you receive?
- How did this affect working relationships?

## Learning Questions

- What did you learn from this experience?
- How has it changed your future approach or influenced other teams?
- What would you do differently next time?
