# TMAY Examples by Career Stage (Senior, Manager)


Senior and manager TMAY answers should emphasize scope, systems thinking, and how you scale teams
or platforms.

## Senior Engineer Focus

- Lead with architecture or scaling work across teams.
- Emphasize impact on reliability, performance, or productivity.
- Highlight mentoring or technical leadership.

## People Manager Focus

- Show team outcomes, culture building, and organizational growth.
- Highlight delivery of business-critical systems.
- Mention how you build environments where others succeed.
