# Conflict Story Example: Data Quality vs Timeline


This example shows how to quantify technical risk in business terms and find a compromise that
protects both quality and speed.

## Core Conflict

- Business pressure to ship fast.
- Technical risk of high false positives.

## Key Moves

- Quantified the impact with a quick analysis.
- Proposed a timeboxed data-cleaning plan.
- Suggested conservative launch thresholds.

## Why it Works

The story shows empathy for business pressure while still protecting the user experience. It
frames the conflict as a joint problem to solve rather than a personal standoff.
