Conflict resolution is one of the strongest indicators of seniority. Interviewers use these
stories to judge maturity, judgment, and cultural fit.

## Why This Question Matters

It reveals how you handle disagreement in high-stakes situations and whether you can prioritize
the organization over ego.

## Frame Conflict As Productive

Modern teams treat conflict as a path to better decisions. Show that you can disagree directly,
use evidence, and land on outcomes that are healthier for the product.
