# Example Questions by Topic

Use these as templates to spark your own questions.

## Team

- What is the team's near-term focus and why?
- How does roadmap planning work?

## Role

- Why is this role open?
- What does success look like in the first six months?

## Company

- How does this team's work connect to the mission?
- What major bets is the company making?

## Manager

- How would your team describe your management style?
- How have you helped someone grow recently?

## Partners

- How does this team collaborate with PM, design, or other engineering teams?
