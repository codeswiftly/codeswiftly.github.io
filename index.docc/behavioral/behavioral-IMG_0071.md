# Leadership and Conflict Prompt Categories


Senior behavioral rounds often focus on leadership situations and conflict navigation. Build stories
that map to these categories.

## Common Conflict Prompts

- Disagreement over implementation or process choices.
- Pushback on scope, time, or cost.
- Stakeholder dissatisfaction with quality.
- Dependency resistance from partner teams.
- Resistance to new tools or processes.

## Common Leadership Situations

- Underperformance or missed deadlines.
- Burnout or work-life balance issues.
- Delivering difficult feedback or reviews.
- Managing morale during change.
- Aligning team members to the right projects.
- Internal disagreement on technical direction.
