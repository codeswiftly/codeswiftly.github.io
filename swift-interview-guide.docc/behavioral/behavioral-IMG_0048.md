# Resource Allocation Conflict: Options and Timeboxing

When leadership requests a critical resource, respond with data and alternatives rather than a
flat no.

## High-signal Moves

- Quantify the impact of losing the resource.
- Offer multiple alternatives with pros and cons.
- Timebox an interim plan and reassess quickly.
- Make tradeoffs visible to leadership.
