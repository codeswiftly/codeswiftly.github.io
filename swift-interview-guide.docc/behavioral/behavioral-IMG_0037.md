# Conflict Resolution Playbook

The best conflict stories show a sequence of actions that led to clarity and maintained
relationships.

## High-signal Actions

- Choose the right communication channel for the moment.
- Demonstrate empathy before pushing your position.
- Maintain composure under emotional pressure.
- Use data or prototypes to ground the decision.
- Involve the right people at the right time.
- Timebox analysis so the conflict does not stall delivery.
- Land a clear resolution and path forward.
- Preserve relationships after the decision.
